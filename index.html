Immunity Board Game

Rules to play this game are as follows:-

  • Each player can move 3 spaces horizontally or vertically.
  • Each player can earn their strength by collecting different weapons.
  • Default Weapon for each player is hammer, which has 10 points.
  • Choose the weapon with strongest value as your strength is directly proportional to your opponent's damage.
  • After getting ready for fight(by collecting weapons), you can choose attack or defend.
  • Try your luck to win!!
